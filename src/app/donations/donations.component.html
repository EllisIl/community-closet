How Donations Work

We appreciate your generous contributions! Here's how you can help our community by donating items to the Community Closet.

Step 1: Choose Your Donation

Select the items you'd like to donate. We accept gently used clothing, shoes, and accessories. If you are unsure whether an item qualifies, feel free to contact us or check the donation guidelines on our website.

Step 2: Drop-Off Location

You can drop off your donations at our designated donation center. The location and hours will be listed on our events page, or you can check the donation schedule for upcoming events where we will accept donations.
